Cambrian ecosystems

This session comprises a workshop exploring Cambrian ecosystems, followed by a consolidation and an opportunity to progress with the Wenlock assignment.

The session should help you to revise concepts from earlier in the course, and guide your approach to the Wenlock assignment.
